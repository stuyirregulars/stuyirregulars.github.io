Mystery

The Stuyvesant Baker Street Irregulars (TSBSI) engage in mystery and thoughtful discussion. We discuss mystery novels, debating authors.

A LITTLE ABOUT OUR CLUB

The Stuyvesant Baker Street Irregulars welcome anyone and will not discriminate based on race, gender, or sexuality. We have an entirely open atmosphere that is designed to provide some fun to relieve stress.

This club is relatively new, and we hope you can help us grow, and become a club full of people who genuinely have fun, and pursue what they want to do.

JOIN AND EXPAND BOUNDARIES

To achieve real change, we have to expand boundaries. Because the Wild West of what-could-be is unexplored but rife with opportunity.



ONE CLUB, MANY VOICES.


We are a united club of people who do not squash anybody, but allow anybody to speak up, to suggest, and to better our club. Nothing is "set" and better methods stay.

A club is something that leads to personal relationships, and the fostering of friends, since both have stuff in common. Couldn't hurt, right?

A club elevates its members and helps relieve the stress that comes when you take 10 periods of classes, all of which go at a fast pace.
